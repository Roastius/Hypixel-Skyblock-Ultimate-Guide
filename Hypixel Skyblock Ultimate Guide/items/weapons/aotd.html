Aspect of the Dragons

The aspect of the end is one of the most used weapons for new players. Coming in at just 150k, the aspect of the end, or the aote, is used even by late game players for it's special ability: To teleport 8 blocks in front of you. This ability makes travelling around the islands much faster and easier, at the cost of 50 mana.

It's special reforge is the warped stone, which can be applied to the AOTE granting it +165 damage and +165 strength. With a full set of strong dragon armor, it buffs the AOTE by +75 damage, 2+ blocks of teleport distance, +3 seconds of the speed bonus, and +5 strength after teleporting.

Aspect of the Void

At enderman slayer VI, you can upgrade the aspect of the end to an aspect of the void, granting it +20 damage and reducing the base mana cost by 5, from 50 to 45. It requires 32 null ovoids, and an AOTE.

Etherwarp Upgrade

The AOTE can be combined with an etherwarp conduit and an etherwarp merger to grant it the etherwarp conduit's ability when teleporting while sneaking.

Trivia

Using the sword's ability just before hitting the ground reduces and fall damage. A completely maxed out AOTE, (Warped reforge, full strong dragon armor, lvl 100 legendary enderman, all hot and fuming potato books) The AOTE deals 420 damage and 295 strength after using the teleport ability. The teleport ability does not work above y level 256. Upon trying to teleport further, a message will appear that says, "There are blocks in the way!" This is the result of a bug where you would teleport 256 blocks downwards if you used it above y level 256.
