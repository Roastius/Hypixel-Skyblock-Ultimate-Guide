The Auction and How to Auction Flip

< Part Two: The Bazaar

The Auction House is a very important feature in Hypixel Skyblock. There you can buy most of the items you will need throughout the entire game. When you go into the auction house, you can bid on auctions, and you can create auctions. There are two options when bidding for items. The first option, (and the most used) is BIN. (Buy It Now) It is exactly what the title says. You can buy it right away, without needing to wait for the auction to end, and without the danger of someone else bidding on the same item. The other option is the standard auction. You can bid a certain amount on an item, and you will most likely have to compete with other players who are trying to get the same item.

When creating an auction, you can choose what kind of auction it is, (BIN or Standard) and you can choose how long the auction will be up for, as well as the starting price of the item. Sometimes if you get lucky, you can get much more money from standard auction than BIN, if many people are competing for your item.

You don't have to worry that much about money when starting off, as it will come naturally through game progression. If you want to get items quickly, then auction flipping is the way to go.

You are now ready for the part in the tutorial. Get at least more than 50k coins before moving on to the last step.
