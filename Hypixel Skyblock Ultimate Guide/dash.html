The Massive World of Skyblock...

Hypixel Skyblock is one of the most popular games in Hypixel, and is a presistent minigame, where there is no objective and no end to the game. Instead, players try to get the best possible weapons, armor, and gear in the game. Some people don't play the game due to the complexity of it, so that's why I made this guide to help newer players (and pros) progress into the game to become a really good Skyblock player. This guide will cover all of the features that Skyblock has to offer, such as mining, dungeons, pets, and much more. Enjoy reading through this guide (that is also my school project) And have fun in Skyblock!
